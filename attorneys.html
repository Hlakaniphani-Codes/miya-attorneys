CEO & Founder — LLB UKZN

Nokwazi B. Miya

Admitted Attorney of the High Court of South Africa

Nokwazi Miya established Miya Attorneys in 2015 with a fierce vision for legal transformation and flawless client representation. Holding an LLB from the University of KwaZulu-Natal, she possesses deep domain expertise in constitutional litigation, strategic legislative drafting, and high-stakes corporate advisory.

Nokwazi has regularly developed custom bylaws, compliance frameworks, and critical subordinate legislation for major municipalities and public entities. She commands an esteemed presence appearing regularly before the High Court and the Constitutional Court of South Africa.

As a proud female black-owned firm maintaining an impeccable Level 1 BBBEE rating, her practice remains uncompromisingly committed to premium, results-oriented execution — acting strictly as Your Legal Remedy.
