About Miya Attorneys

Strategic Legal Solutions. Trusted Partnerships. Lasting Impact.

Who We Are

Strategic Legal Solutions.
Trusted Partnerships. Lasting Impact.

Founded in 2015, Miya Attorneys is a boutique law firm delivering strategic, solution-driven legal services to individuals, businesses, public institutions and corporate clients.

Headquartered in Durban with a growing presence in Gauteng, Mpumalanga and Cape Town. Serving clients across South Africa with professionalism and excellence.
We advise on complex legal and commercial matters, providing practical, commercially informed solutions that protect interests and support sound decision-making.
Exceptional representation begins with understanding our clients. We invest time to appreciate each client's objectives, challenges and operating environment.
Proactive approach: We identify, manage and minimise legal risk before it becomes a costly problem. Preventing challenges while remaining ready to provide decisive representation.
Wholly black-owned with Level 1 B-BBEE recognition. Committed to transformation, excellence and inclusive economic participation.

Guided by integrity, accountability, innovation and excellence. We help our clients build stronger businesses, institutions and communities.

Vision Statement

To be a leading and most preferred legal resource in Africa with the ability to provide a wholly integrated high quality law service to all its clients through diligence, trust, mutual respect and enhancement of human potential.

Mission Statement

To provide quality legal solutions for our diverse clients. As a growing firm, we are committed to looking beyond the obvious. While we pay close attention to detail, we always consider the bigger picture.

Our Foundation

Firm's Composition & Experience

Miya Attorneys Pty Ltd was established in March 2015 by its female director and owner, Nokwazi B. Miya, through a wide range of experience in the legal services industry and the ability to manage and execute complex legal instructions and find innovative solutions to intricate legal problems.

With a base in KwaZulu-Natal, Gauteng, and Mpumalanga, we are able to deliver excellent professional services to meet our client's needs. The firm is wholly black owned and has Level One BBBEE procurement recognition.
