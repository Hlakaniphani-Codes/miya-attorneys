About Miya Attorneys

A boutique law firm dedicated to providing exceptional legal services with integrity, innovation, and unwavering commitment to our clients.

Who We Are

A Legacy of Legal Excellence

Miya Attorneys is a boutique law firm providing legal services to a wide spectrum of private, public, and corporate entities. We have offices in Richards Bay and satellite offices in Gauteng and Mpumalanga, resulting in an extensive and wide ranging client base.

Our values and philosophy is to render dedicated legal services to our clients. As a firm that provides legal solutions, we believe it is important to invest the time to thoroughly understand our clients and view our engagement as a partnership.

"The good lawyer is not the man who has an eye to every side and angle of contingency, but who throws himself on your part so heartily, that he can get you out of a scrape."

Setting us apart from most traditional legal service providers and law firms is the fact that our services are designed to be "fire preventing" rather than "fire fighting". We play an important role in risk identification, risk management, and risk minimisation.

We have qualified and experienced professionals and can claim perfection in work through continuous struggle and hard work. We understand the legal philosophy and have human capital that is dynamic, adaptable, and conversant with most aspects of the law.

Our Foundation

Firm's Composition & Experience

Miya Attorneys Pty Ltd was established in March 2015 by its female director and owner, Nokwazi B. Miya, through a wide range of experience in the legal services industry and the ability to manage and execute complex legal instructions and find innovative solutions to intricate legal problems.

With a base in KwaZulu-Natal, Gauteng, and Mpumalanga, we are able to deliver excellent professional services to meet our client's needs. The firm is wholly black owned and has Level One BBBEE procurement recognition.
